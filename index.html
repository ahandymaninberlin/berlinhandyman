SERVICES

We provide a wide range of handyman, renovation and repair services across Berlin:

  • Furniture assembly (IKEA etc.)
  • Custom carpentry, furniture design and installation
  • Furniture and trim restoration and up-cycling
  • Wall mounting (TVs, mirrors, shelves, artwork, curtain rails)
  • Painting and decorating
  • Small renovations
  • Drywall repairs and plaster patching
  • Tiling and tile repairs
  • Silicone and sealing work
  • Flooring repairs and laminate installation
  • Kitchen installations and modifications
  • Backsplash and worksurface fitting
  • Bathroom repairs and fittings
  • Door hanging and adjustment
  • Door and hinge repairs
  • Wardrobe and storage/shelving builds
