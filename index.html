SERVICES & AREAS

SERVICES

We provide a wide range of handyman, maintenance, and light renovation services across Berlin. This includes furniture assembly (IKEA and custom builds), custom furniture, carpentry, wall mounting (TVs, mirrors, shelves, artwork, curtain rails), painting and decorating, small renovations, drywall repairs, plaster patching, tiling and tile repairs, silicone and sealing work, flooring repairs and laminate installation, kitchen installations and worksurface fitting, bathroom repairs and fittings, door hanging and adjustment, lock and hinge repairs, wardrobe and shelving builds, and general carpentry. We also handle light plumbing tasks such as leaking taps, replacing fittings, installing sinks, sealing showers, and fixing minor pipe issues. Light electrical work includes replacing light fixtures, installing lamps, switches, sockets, and mounting appliances (non-intrusive work only). Additional services include property maintenance, landlord and tenant repairs, Airbnb turnover repairs, move-in/move-out fixes, end-of-tenancy touch-ups, furniture modifications, flat-pack adjustments, drilling and installation, waste removal, and general odd jobs around the home or office.
